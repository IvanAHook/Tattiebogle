All Textures shall be in the folder "textures"
All fbx/obj shall be in the folder "Fbx"
All Shaders shall be in folder "Shaders"
All materials Shall be in folder "materials"
All prefabs shall be in folder "prefabs" - This folder contains subfolders to make it more easy for the designers to select the specific prefab!


Naming conventions

scripts: MyScript.cs

shader: Name_Shdr

materials: name_M

meshes:  Name_Mesh.fbx

Prefabs: Name_Pfb

Diffuse: Name_T
Normal: Name_N
Spec: Name_S
